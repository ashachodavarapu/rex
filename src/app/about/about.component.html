Our Story

Started as a premium food group, Rex Group was born from the passion to bring nutritious healthy delicacies featuring sweet potatoes, sweet corn, groundnuts and healthy bowl varieties to health-conscious food lovers.

We understand the demand for quality, fresh, and nutritious food options. That's why we created Rex Group to deliver premium healthy comfort foods with wholesome ingredients, right to your doorstep.

Today, we proudly serve hundreds of customers across India, delivering not just food, but premium nutrition and exceptional taste in every bite.
