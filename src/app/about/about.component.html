About Rex

Your trusted partner for authentic, homestyle Indian meals delivered fresh daily

Our Story

Started as a small family business, Rex was born from the passion to bring authentic homestyle Indian cooking to busy professionals and families who miss the taste of home-cooked meals.

We understand the challenges of modern life - long working hours, busy schedules, and the struggle to maintain a healthy diet. That's why we created Rex to bridge this gap with our daily tiffin service.

Today, we proudly serve hundreds of customers across the city, delivering not just food, but the warmth and comfort of home-cooked meals.
