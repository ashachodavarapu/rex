Our Story

Rex is built on the foundation of leadership and excellence. Our Food Truck Division specializes in premium healthy delicacies featuring sweet potatoes, sweet corn, groundnuts and nutritious bowl varieties for health-conscious customers.

As a company, Rex operates across multiple sectors with our food truck business being one of our key divisions. We deliver quality, innovation, and exceptional service in everything we do.

Today, Rex continues to expand its business portfolio while our food truck division serves hundreds of customers across India, delivering premium nutrition and exceptional taste in every meal.
